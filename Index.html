    Programs

    Dan Zan Ryu JuJitsu

    Dan Zan Ryu Jujitsu is a modern American system of jujitsu that blends traditional Japanese jujitsu techniques with elements from judo, karate, kung fu, Hawaiian Lua, boxing, and wrestling. The system emphasizes self-defense, throws, joint locks, escapes, and healing arts like massage

    Brazilian JuJitsu

    Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) is a martial art and combat sport focused on ground fighting and submission grappling. Developed from Japanese jujitsu and judo, BJJ emphasizes technique, leverage, and positioning to control opponents and apply submissions such as joint locks and chokes, making it effective for smaller practitioners against larger opponents. It is widely practiced for self-defense, sport competition, and mixed martial arts (MMA).

    Judo

    Judo is a Japanese martial art and Olympic sport that focuses on throws, pins, joint locks, and chokes to subdue an opponent, emphasizing technique, balance, and leverage over brute strength.

    Capoeira

    Capoeira is an Afro-Brazilian martial art that combines elements of dance, acrobatics, music, and combat. Characterized by fluid, rhythmic movements, kicks, sweeps, and dodges it emphasizes creativity, agility, and strategy over direct confrontation.

    Striking

    Striking is a form of combat that focuses on hitting an opponent using punches, kicks, elbows, and knees. Striking emphasizes timing, speed, accuracy, and technique to deliver effective blows while avoiding or blocking attacks.
